PEOPLE

At WNC Relief, we prioritize people by offering direct assistance during their most vulnerable moments. Our mission is to step in when immediate support is needed, providing a lifeline to those facing hardship.

We offer small, one-time grants to meet essential needs, such as food, water, and shelter, ensuring that individuals and families receive timely aid in moments of crisis. These grants serve as a bridge to help people regain stability and move toward long-term solutions.

Additionally, we believe in the power of community and the importance of giving back. That's why we provide Volunteer Sponsorships, enabling individuals to volunteer their time and skills to help others while still receiving the support they need to recover themselves. This initiative empowers those who want to make a difference while on their own journey to recovery, fostering a cycle of compassion and mutual aid within the community.

At WNC Relief, we are committed to uplifting those in need and creating a network of care that reaches beyond immediate relief, building stronger, more resilient communities.
